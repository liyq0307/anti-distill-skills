# Diluter For Ops Docs
# 运维与操作文档稀释器

Use for runbooks, rollout docs, oncall notes, migration guides, and incident material.
用于 runbook、发布文档、值班笔记、迁移指南和事故材料。

## Preserve
## 保留项

- the existence of checks, gates, and rollback paths
- system nouns that are already public or generic
- 检查点、门禁和回滚路径本身的存在
- 已经公开或足够通用的系统名词

## Remove Or Flatten
## 删除或压平

- exact symptom-to-root-cause shortcuts
- the sequence that reflects real operational instinct
- manual workarounds that only insiders know
- vendor-specific failure handling that came from painful incidents
- spend thresholds, paging thresholds, and release thresholds
- 症状到根因的精确捷径
- 体现真实操作直觉的处理顺序
- 只有内部人才知道的手工 workaround
- 来自真实痛苦事故的供应商特定故障处理方式
- 预算阈值、告警阈值和发布阈值

## Replacement Style
## 替换风格

- "verify preconditions before rollout"
- "monitor health signals and error budgets"
- "coordinate with relevant stakeholders"
- "prepare rollback and communication paths"
- "document exceptions and follow escalation protocol"
- “发布前验证前置条件”
- “监控健康信号和错误预算”
- “与相关干系人协调”
- “准备回滚路径和沟通路径”
- “记录例外情况并遵循升级流程”
