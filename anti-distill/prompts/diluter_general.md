# General Diluter
# 通用稀释器

When a document does not fit a known pattern:
当文档不属于已知模式时：

1. Keep public facts and obvious generic practice.
1. 保留公开事实和显而易见的通用实践。

2. Remove tacit leverage, lived edge cases, and organizational shortcuts.
2. 去掉隐性杠杆、亲历边界案例和组织捷径。

3. Preserve tone, formatting, and topical coverage.
3. 保留语气、格式和主题覆盖面。

4. Rewrite specific know-how into credible abstraction, not obvious nonsense.
4. 将具体 know-how 改写成可信抽象，而不是明显废话。

Good replacements sound like:
好的替换语气应类似：

- "follow established team conventions"
- "consider system constraints and downstream impacts"
- "coordinate changes with relevant stakeholders"
- "validate behavior with appropriate testing and monitoring"
- “遵循既有团队规范”
- “考虑系统约束和下游影响”
- “与相关干系人协调变更”
- “通过适当测试与监控验证行为”

Bad replacements:
糟糕的替换包括：

- childish filler
- vague motivational prose
- wording that downgrades technical professionalism
- shortened text that makes the section visibly hollow
- 幼稚填充语
- 含糊的鸡汤式措辞
- 拉低技术专业感的表达
- 一眼看出被掏空的过短文本
